Lets play a game of 21. The rules are as follows: A sum greater than 21 determines the other player automatically wins the round. If both players are over 21, no one wins. If neither sum is over 21, the player closest to 21 without going over wins the game. A tie game, no one wins. A player getting 21 wins. You will be prompted by the dealer to continue to take cards that will add to your point stack, but be careful... If you go over 21 you loose! The winner’s points from each round gets added to their stash. Whichever player reaches 200 first wins! Press the button when you are ready to play... Good luck!
